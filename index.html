The Future of Investing

Varying reward system

The system is designed to be sustainable and fair to every investor. It encourages positive activities that benefit everyone and the ecosystem moving forward. We will have a unique and variable rewards program.

Daily ROI De-Fi game

Who said we can't have fun while investing? Optimum Fund will release a DeFi game based on providing daily returns to its users. Staked tokens in The Orchard will earn a daily return of 2%.

Deposit and Compounding Taxes

No deposit taxes will be levied on the users, and investors will also enjoy zero tax while compounding. Low and favorable taxes are a key point in the ecosystem. Buy and sell taxes will be fixed throughout the ecosystem.

Incubation and Tutorial program

Revenues from tutorial programs will feed the marketing wallet, while revenues from the incubation program will be injected back into the ecosystem. The fees are designed to have a positive impact on the ecosystem.

THE

CHEST

The OMF Token, the native token of the ecosystem, will be staked in The Chest. This platform aims to refine traditional high yield staking methods, which are often inflationary, into a sustainable yet profitable protocol. OMF staking aims to provide complete value on investment to its users through an outstanding and lasting rewards system.

To reward our holders and stakers, OMF staking will have a tiered system. There will be no staking taxes for deposits and withdrawals of OMF. For more comprehensive details about The Chest, please refer to the whitepaper.

PHASE I

Strategic campaigns kickstart. Aggressive marketing via various platforms, including social media platforms. Introducing the Optimum Fund ecosystem.

PHASE VI

Optimum Fund is deployed on the Avalanche C-Chain network. More campaigns, partnerships, and marketing efforts ensue. Contracts are deployed and audited by third-party companies. The ecosystem commences.
